Morning Fuel: The 'Ice Americano' Culture ☕

Cultural Context
🧊 'Eoljuk-a': "Even if I freeze to death, I will drink Iced Americano."
☕ Local Chains: Mega Coffee, Ediya, Paik Coffee, Twosome Place
💡 TIP: Skip Starbucks (Expensive) → Try local chains!
🥪 Breakfast: Isaac Toast or Egg Drop
How to Order at a Kiosk
  1. 1Switch Language (Top Right)
  2. 2Navigate Categories — Red labels = Recommended
  3. 3Customize — Suggest 30% Sugar
  4. 4Checkout — 'To Go' = you cannot stay inside
  5. 5Cleanup — Return tray to station
Key Phrases:
Iced Americano, please 아이스 아메리카노 한잔 주세요
To go 포장 해 주세요 · Po-jang hae ju-se-yo
Eat here 여기서 먹어요 · Yeo-gi-seo meok-eo-yo

Master the Commute: Subway & Bus 🚇

📱 Apps: Naver Map + Kakao Map — Use for elevator exits & exact door numbers
🚇
Subway
  • Check Direction — Entrances are often separate
  • Get Refunds via Deposit machine for single-trip cards
  • Check T-money balance at machines
🚌
Bus
  • No Food or Drink onboard
  • Press Stop button BEFORE your station
  • Check the route number carefully
🤫
Etiquette
  • Silence is golden — keep voice low
  • Respect 🩷 Pink (Pregnant) seats
  • Respect 💛 Yellow (Elderly) seats
🕵️ Lost?
Look for someone in ALL BLACK. Statistically, that's a local.
🔧 Troubleshooting
Check T-money balance at ticket machines or convenience stores.
⛴️ Bonus
Hangang "Bus" Ferry — 3,000 KRW scenic river crossing!

Recovery & Important Warnings ⚠️

Option A 🚴
Hangang Biker
Rent a bike at the river and cycle along the Hangang paths. Peaceful, scenic, and great exercise after a big meal.
Option B 🛁
Jjimjilbang 찜질방
Korean spa sauna. Wear the Sheep Head Towel (Yang-meo-ri). Try Sikhye + Smoked Eggs. Bring a charging cable!
⚠️ Important Warnings
🚨
Cults
Ignore overly friendly strangers asking about "culture" or "spirit" — it's a recruitment tactic.
🚭
Rules
No smoking while walking. No drunk cycling. Many shops close 8PM.
📷
Privacy
Blur strangers' faces in photos before sharing publicly.
📱
Alerts
Emergency text alarm sounds are usually just weather or safety updates. Don't panic.
