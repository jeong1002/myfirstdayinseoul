# VEWVEW Brand Encyclopedia (Context for Claude)

## 1. Brand Identity
* **Name**: VEWVEW
* **Core Business**: A one-stop K-Beauty booking platform specifically designed for inbound tourists visiting South Korea.
* **Service Scope**: Dermatology, Dental services, Personal Color Analysis, Premium SPA, etc.
* **Revenue Model**: B2B2C model. We connect international customers with local clinics/studios and earn a commission fee once the customer completes their paid service on-site.

## 2. Business Logic & Value Chain
* **For Clinics (Partners)**: We act as a marketing agency. We bring in influencers for on-site experiences to boost the clinic's international visibility and reputation.
* **For Influencers**: We provide free or discounted premium K-Beauty experiences in exchange for promotional video content on social media (Instagram, TikTok, etc.).
* **For Tourists (Customers)**: We provide a transparent, professional, and easy-to-book platform that solves language barriers and information asymmetry.


## 3. Social Media Strategy (Instagram)
Our current Instagram content strategy is divided into three main pillars:
1.  **Memes**: Humorous content related to Korean culture, travel "pain points," or beauty-related relatability to drive engagement and virality.
2.  **Educational/Professional**: Deep dives into treatment recommendation, and the latest Korean beauty trends.
3.  **"Everything Korea" Guide**: Acting as a "Korea Expert" by introducing food, hidden spots, and lifestyle tips to build trust and authority.


## 4. Target Audience
* **Primary**: International female tourists (20-35) interested in K-Beauty.
* **Key Pain Points**: Price transparency, language barrier, fear of "foreigner tax," recovery time concerns.

## 5. Social Media Strategy 
* **Pillar 1: Memes (Relatability)**
  * *Goal*: Virality & reach. 
  * *Topics*: Travel struggles, K-beauty obsession, expectation vs reality.
* **Pillar 2: Educational (Authority)**
  * *Goal*: Trust & savings.
  * *Topics*: how to choose Rejuran, Juvelook guidebook, small budgt big glow
* **Pillar 3: "Korea Expert" (Community)**
  * *Goal*: Retention.
  * *Topics*: Olive Young hauls, T-money tips, Seoul's beauty neighborhood guide.

## 6. Specific Content Guideline
* **CTAs**: "Link in bio to book," "Comment 'GLOW' for price list."
* **Hooks**: Start with a question (e.g., "Stop doing your skin care wrong...",flying korean for famous glassy skin, Why settle for 1 + 1 = 2, Mirror, mirror on the wall... Why are my pores so visible? ).
